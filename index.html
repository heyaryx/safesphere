About SafeSphere

SafeSphere was created to address the growing concern of inappropriate content exposure to teens online. As parents ourselves, we understand the delicate balance between protecting young users and respecting their independence.

Our extension works by analyzing web page content, filtering search results, and blocking access to known harmful websites, all while providing parents with oversight tools that don't feel invasive to teens.

Developed by Aryx, SafeSphere focuses on creating a safer internet experience that grows with your teen and adapts to their changing needs.

Easy Installation

1. Download

Clone or download the SafeSphere repository

2. Access Extensions

Navigate to Chrome extensions (chrome://extensions/)

3. Enable Developer Mode

Toggle on "Developer mode" in the top-right corner

4. Load Extension

Click "Load unpacked" and select the SafeSphere directory

5. Complete

The extension is now installed and ready to protect your teen
